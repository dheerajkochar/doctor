Treatment Process

From consultation to beautiful results - our streamlined process

Dental Implants Process

1

Consultation & Assessment

Comprehensive examination, X-rays, and 3D CT scans to evaluate bone quality

2

Implant Placement

Surgical placement of titanium implant under local anesthesia - typically 20-30 mins

3

Osseointegration

Implant bonds with jawbone over 3-6 months (varies per patient)

4

Crown Placement

Custom-made crown attached to implant for natural appearance and function

Wisdom Tooth Extraction Process

1

Evaluation

X-rays and examination to assess tooth position and extraction complexity

2

Anesthesia

Local or IV sedation for comfortable, pain-free procedure

3

Extraction

Gentle removal of tooth with minimal trauma (15-45 mins depending on complexity)

4

Recovery & Care

Post-op instructions and gauze packing, usually heals within 1-2 weeks

Composite Bonding Process

1

Shade Selection

Choose the perfect shade to match your natural teeth color

2

Surface Preparation

Tooth surface cleaned and lightly etched for better material adhesion

3

Bonding Application

Composite resin carefully applied and shaped to tooth contour

4

Curing & Polishing

Light-hardened and polished for natural shine - completes in single visit

Crown & Bridge Process

1

Preparation

Damaged tooth or teeth prepared by removing decay and reshaping surface

2

Impression Taking

Precise digital scan or impression sent to lab for custom crown/bridge creation

3

Temporary Crown

Temporary crown placed while permanent one is being fabricated (1-2 weeks)

4

Final Placement

Permanent crown securely cemented with perfect bite adjustment

Invisible Aligners Process

1

3D Scan

Digital scan of teeth creates 3D model showing complete treatment plan

2

Aligner Creation

Custom-made clear aligners fabricated in precise phases for gradual movement

3

Regular Wear

Wear aligners 20-22 hours daily, change to next set every 1-2 weeks

4

Retention

Wear retainers after treatment to maintain beautiful straight smile

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common dental concerns

How long do dental implants last?

+

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last 25-30 years or even a lifetime. Regular brushing, flossing, and dental checkups are essential for longevity.

Is wisdom tooth extraction necessary?

+

Not always. Extraction is recommended if the tooth is impacted, causing pain, damaging adjacent teeth, or creating hygiene issues. Our dentist will assess your specific case.

Are invisible aligners as effective as braces?

+

Yes, clear aligners are very effective for most orthodontic cases. They provide comparable results to traditional braces with added convenience and aesthetic appeal. Complex cases may still require traditional braces.

How much does teeth whitening cost?

+

Can composite bonding fix cavities?

+

Yes, composite bonding is an excellent solution for small to medium-sized cavities. The resin material is tooth-colored and provides a natural appearance while restoring tooth function.

What payment options do you accept?

+

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments (UPI, Net Banking). We also offer EMI options for major treatments. Please contact us for specific payment plan details.
